Road Safety Tips

  • Always wear your seatbelt and ensure all passengers do too.
  • Obey speed limits and traffic signals.
  • Avoid distractions such as texting or using your phone while driving.
  • Maintain a safe following distance from other vehicles.
  • Use turn signals to indicate your intentions to other drivers.

Road Safety Resources

If you need assistance or want to learn more about road safety, consider reaching out to:

  • Your local transportation department or traffic police
  • Road safety organizations or agencies
  • Driving schools or defensive driving courses
  • Online road safety guides or resources
