Child Safety Tips

  • Teach children about stranger danger and how to respond in different situations.
  • Set clear rules about internet safety and supervise children's online activities.
  • Ensure that children know their full name, address, and phone number in case of emergencies.
  • Teach children to always wear helmets when riding bikes or participating in other wheeled activities.
  • Regularly check toys and playground equipment for any potential hazards.

Resources

If you need assistance or want to learn more about child safety, consider reaching out to:

  • Local child protection agencies or organizations
  • Child helplines or hotlines
  • Parenting support groups
  • Online resources and forums
