Women Safety Tips

  • Avoid walking alone at night, especially in poorly lit areas.
  • Trust your instincts and be aware of your surroundings.
  • Keep emergency contacts programmed into your phone.
  • Learn self-defense techniques to protect yourself if needed.
  • Always let someone know your plans and when you expect to return.

Resources

If you are in danger or need assistance, consider reaching out to:

  • Emergency services: Dial 911
  • Local women's shelters or support organizations
  • Women's helplines or hotlines
  • Community support groups
