Fire Safety Tips

  • Install smoke alarms in every room and test them regularly.
  • Create a fire escape plan and practice it with your family.
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.
  • Store matches and lighters out of reach of children.
  • Never leave cooking or candles unattended.

Fire Safety Resources

If you need assistance or want to learn more about fire safety, consider reaching out to:

  • Your local fire department
  • Fire safety organizations or agencies
  • Community fire safety programs
  • Online fire safety courses or resources
